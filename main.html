My Journey

Before — 105 kg

After — 70 kg

It all started in 2011 when I struggled to keep up with my daughter. Once a college fitness enthusiast, years of corporate life had pushed me over 108 kg. That moment reignited my passion for fitness.

I began small — swimming, cycling, badminton — until running found me in 2013 at Cubbon Park. By 2015, joining a club at Bangalore University taught me the true power of the tribe — that belonging fuels consistency.

Since then, I’ve completed 8 full marathons with a personal best of 3:39, along with numerous 5Ks, 10Ks, and half-marathons. Through every step, I’ve learned that progress lives in habits, consistency, and community.

🏃 “Fitness isn’t a destination — it’s who you become on the way.”
